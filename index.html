About Me

As a New York native, I live a fast paced life with a Metrocard in one hand and a cup of coffee in the other. Born to lead and built to succeed. Constantly developing and discovering new markets to enter. My drive in life is fueled by my passion for developing new technical skills to help my local community. I hope to utilize these skills to eventually move into international markets assist in global initiatives.


After 3+ years in multiple hospitality environments nurturing client relationships and building brand awareness. I now have the opportunity to marry my exceptional interpersonal skills with my newly refined technical skills. After 4 months in an immersive Python bootcamp, I have emerged with a stronger understanding of Python and Angular.Js programming languages, computational thinking, problem solving skills, and fundamentals of qualitative research. As the Program Coordinator for the The Knowledge House, I analyze and concatenate student data with corresponding datasets. This enabled my organization to visualize student data and development. This pursuit is one of the necessary stepping stones in becoming a Full Stack Developer.

Some people are born with very little; some are fortunate enough to have it all.
When I grew up, we didn't have much. I had to hustle to get what I wanted... but I had that hunger for more.
I didn't always make the right choices, but I learned from my mistakes.
